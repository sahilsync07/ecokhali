About Us

At Ecokhali, we celebrate the rich traditions of the Dongaria tribe of Odisha, known for their exceptional craftsmanship in creating sustainable leaf-made products. Our plates and bowls are not just eco-friendly alternatives—they are a tribute to a community that has perfected the art of weaving nature into daily life. Every product tells a story of heritage, sustainability, and the harmonious relationship between people and nature.

We are committed to promoting eco-conscious living by offering products that are both beautiful and biodegradable. Join us in supporting the Dongaria tribe and embracing a lifestyle that cares for our planet.
